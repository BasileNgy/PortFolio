Buddy Arena

Developper at Exkee
2023 - Unity

Buddy Arena is a fast-paced MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) game set in the magical NEXUS World. Players, known as Crushers, team up with their quirky Buddies, each possessing unique abilities and combat styles. The game features intense 2-3 minute battles where strategy and choice of Buddies, weapons, and abilities are crucial

This project has been distributed by Affyn, originally created as a minigame, it became progressively the main game during developement. Buddy Arena take place in a metaverse, as intented, players are able to pay for loots through NFT and cryptocurrency. On this project, I worked as UI/UX developper, so I created UI structures and menu screens.

  • Work Done:
    • Use of Playfab database to manage players inventories
    • Use of GraphQL requests to manage players data through Apollo
    • Implementation of a generic cards system, with selection, interaction and sorting
    • Creation of menus interfaces managed with a state machine

Flaunted

Gam Jam
03/2021 - 48h - Unity - 5 persons

The player is chased by a reaper in a dark mansion. They have exactly 30 seconds to move and perform all the actions they want to escape, open a chest, open a door, use an item from their inventory; however, each action costs time. At the end of their turn, the player can no longer move, it is now the reaper's turn to move and try to catch them. The lamp's lighting is proportional to the player's mental resistance state, which varies according to the items used and found.

  • Work Done:
    • Creation and management of animations with Animator Controller
    • Management of movements with a NavMesh Agent
    • Implementation of inventory and item drop rates
    • UI display for obtaining items and integrating their effects
    • UI indicator to know the direction of the reaper (not integrated)

Speller

Personal Project
2021 - Unity

The game is a turn-based duel on a 2D chessboard. The concept is quite similar to Dofus fights, however, the attacks are not immediate, meaning that the spells progress on the chessboard at their own pace. To defeat your opponent, you need to develop a strategy over several turns to dodge the opponent's spells and trap them in yours. I chose to leave the game at the prototype stage to start another project and explore new challenges.
